üß§ Section 1.1.4 & 1.1.5 ‚Äî Accessories

Gloves

Available in Black or Tan. Usually worn under sleeves unless sleeves are rolled. Note: If gloves are added, watches may be partially or completely invisible.

Rolled Sleeves

Almost every British Army uniform has the option for rolled sleeves, except for formals. Rolled sleeves allow for the display of accessories like watches, gloves, and tattoos.

üíÇ Section 1.1.6 ‚Äî Formal Uniforms

No. 1 & No. 2 Dress

Worn by OF-2+. Limited customization: No watches or tattoos. No. 1s use full laid-out medals for ceremonial use, while No. 2s use ribbons. No. 2s also feature a "formal cord" over the left shoulder (Red, Blue, or Orange depending on regiment).

No. 14 Dress

To be worn by OF-6+. Fully allows watches and tattoos. Features customizable belts (e.g., ETS: Blue with chest pin, RMP: Red).

HQ Dress (No. 3)

AEST Gen.+. Allows any pin or patch. Medals are full-size like No. 1s.
