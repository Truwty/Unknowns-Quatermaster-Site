Rank Insignias

Official British Army rank badges and insignia specifications.

Rank Hierarchy Standards

All personnel must display the correct rank insignia according to their current position. Rank badges must be worn in the proper position and maintained in pristine condition at all times.

Officer Ranks

Code Rank Insignia Description
OF-1 Second Lieutenant Entry-level commissioned officer rank.
OF-2 Lieutenant Junior officer rank, typically platoon commander.
OF-3 Captain Company commander or senior staff officer.
OF-4 Major Battalion second-in-command or senior staff position.
OF-5 Lieutenant Colonel Battalion commander or senior regimental officer.
OF-6 Colonel Regimental commander or senior brigade officer.
OF-7 Brigadier Brigade commander or senior divisional officer.
OF-8 Major General Divisional commander or equivalent.
OF-9 Lieutenant General Corps commander or senior operational command.
OF-10 General Highest operational command rank.

Non-Commissioned Ranks

Code Rank Insignia Description
OR-1 Private Basic soldier rank, no insignia required.
OR-2 Lance Corporal Junior non-commissioned officer.
OR-3 Corporal Section commander.
OR-4 Sergeant Platoon sergeant.
OR-5 Staff Sergeant Company sergeant major.
OR-6 Warrant Officer Class 2 Regimental sergeant major.
OR-7 Warrant Officer Class 1 Regimental sergeant major (senior).
